Monstera Deliciosa

The Monstera Deliciosa is one of the most well-known plants in the community. It is very easy to take care of, as it can handle multiple different lighting conditions and lack of water. They also grow quite large, and the bigger they get the more fenestrations they have. This specific variety has an all green leaf but other more rare types of Monstera Deliciosa can have variagation.
